Where builders
ship real things.

$TBD

Hours straight — no breaks required

Builders expected — every major welcome

KeanUHackThis is Kean University’s flagship hackathon — a 48-hour overnight sprint where students from every major and skill level come together to build something real. No prior experience required.

You’ll have access to mentors from industry, dedicated workshop sessions, free food around the clock, and a community of builders who are here to make something that matters.

Whether you’re writing your first line of code or shipping your tenth side project, KeanUHackThis is where you prove what you can build in 48 hours.
